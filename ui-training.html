FAQs - Section 1

Q1. What is this course about?

This course prepares learners for professional UI Design roles using Figma, FigJam, and Behance. You’ll master responsive layouts, design systems, and portfolio presentation.

Q2. Do I need any prior design experience to join?

No prior experience is required. This program starts with UI fundamentals and gradually moves to advanced design workflows.

Q3. Who is this course ideal for?

It’s perfect for students, freshers, graphic designers, or anyone looking to start a career as a UI Designer or Product Designer.

Q4. What is the duration and schedule of the program?

It’s a 2-month (8-week) program with around 60 total hours, conducted 4 days a week for 2 hours/day.

Q5. How can I enroll?

You can enroll through the official UI UX Cloud website or contact the trainer (Ajinkya Kulthe) directly for upcoming batch details.

Q6. Does this course include placement assistance?

Yes, Trivikratech provides career guidance, LinkedIn portfolio review, and interview tips to prepare you for MNC and startup roles.

Q7. Can I add this certificate to my resume or LinkedIn?

Absolutely! The certification is digital and shareable — you can showcase it on LinkedIn, Behance, or your portfolio website.

Q8. What support is provided after course completion?

Post-course, learners get: Access to alumni community Guidance on freelance projects Portfolio improvement feedback

Q9. Is this course online or offline?

The training can be delivered online (live interactive sessions) or offline (studio-based batches) — depending on the schedule.

Q10. What makes this course different from YouTube or self-learning?

This is a guided professional training, not just tutorials. You’ll receive: Structured learning path Practical projects Personal feedback Portfolio and career support

Q11. Which tools will I learn in this course?

You’ll master: Figma – Core UI Design, Auto Layout, and Design Systems FigJam – Brainstorming and Flow Mapping Behance & Dribbble – Portfolio and Case Study Presentation

Q12: Why is UI/UX design important for my business?

A good UI/UX ensures better usability, higher conversions, and long-term customer loyalty.

Q13. Will I learn prototyping and animation?

Yes! Week 6 is fully focused on Interaction Design & Prototyping using Figma’s Smart Animate and Micro-interactions.

Q14. Is coding knowledge required for this course?

No, this is a pure design-focused course — you’ll learn visual design, layout systems, and documentation, not coding.

Q15. Are there hands-on exercises in every week?

Yes, each week includes exercises and mini-projects like responsive screens, design systems, and case studies.

Q16. What is the Capstone Project?

In Week 8, you’ll design a full app UI (3–5 screens, responsive) and present it as your final Behance project.

Q17. Will I receive feedback on my designs?

AYes, you’ll get peer review sessions and trainer feedback to improve your work before the final presentation.

Q18. Do I get a certificate after completing the course?

Yes, you’ll earn a UI Design Professional Certificate (MNC-Level) awarded by UI UX Cloud, after project evaluation and portfolio review.

Q19. How will this course help build my portfolio?

By the end of the course, you’ll have at least 2 mini-projects, 1 case study, and 1 capstone project uploaded on Behance or Dribbble.

Q20. What kind of job roles can I apply for after completing this course?

You’ll be ready for roles such as: Junior UI Designer Product Designer Design System Associate Visual Designer Freelance UI Designer
