FAQs - Graphic Design Course

Q1. I’m completely new to design. Can I still join this course?

Yes! The program starts from the fundamentals and gradually takes you to advanced levels — perfect for complete beginners.

Q2. What is the duration of the Graphic Design Course?

The complete course runs for 3 months, including live classes, assignments, and real-world portfolio projects.

Q3. Which software will I learn during the course?

You’ll master Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and CorelDRAW — plus basics of 3D mockups using Blender or Adobe Dimension.

Q4. Do I need prior design or art background?

No, prior experience isn’t required. You’ll learn everything from design principles to tools and real-world project work.

Q5. Are the classes live or recorded?

All classes are live with interactive mentor sessions, and recordings are provided for later access and revision.

Q6. Will I get practical experience or only theory?

Every module includes hands-on projects — posters, social ads, logos, brochures, and packaging — ensuring full practical exposure.

Q7. Do I get a certificate after completion?

Yes, you’ll receive a Course Completion Certificate and an Internship Letter after successfully submitting your final portfolio.

Q8. What kind of projects will I create?

You’ll design 12+ professional projects such as event posters, brand logos, product ads, packaging, and complete branding kits.

Q9. Is the course available online or offline?

Yes, the course is available in both Live Online and Offline Classroom modes, with flexible hybrid options too.

Q10. What are the system requirements to learn graphic design?

A laptop or desktop with minimum 8GB RAM, i5 processor, and 2GB graphics card is recommended for smooth performance.

Q11. Do I need to buy paid software licenses?

No. You’ll get guidance to use free trial or educational versions of Adobe tools throughout the course.

Q12. Will I learn both print and digital design?

Yes, you’ll learn both — from business cards and brochures to digital ads, packaging, and branding visuals.

Q13. Will I learn how to create 3D mockups?

Yes, you’ll learn how to make professional 3D product mockups and packaging visuals using Blender or Adobe Dimension.

Q14. Can I start freelancing after this course?

Absolutely! You’ll learn how to price your work, handle clients, write proposals, and deliver professional projects.

Q15. What kind of career support will I receive?

You’ll get 1:1 portfolio guidance, resume & interview prep, internship assistance, and freelance project leads.

Q16. Are there any exams or tests?

No written exams — your performance is evaluated through project submissions and creative consistency.

Q17. What if I miss a live class?

All sessions are recorded and made available in your student dashboard for unlimited replay and review.

Q18. Can I include my course projects in my portfolio?

Yes! All your course projects and final assignments can be used in your personal or Behance portfolio.

Q19. What career options do I have after this course?

You can work as a Graphic Designer, Branding Designer, Print Artist, Packaging Designer, or Freelance Visual Creator.

Q20. How do I enroll in the Graphic Design Course?

Click on “Get Free Counselling” or “Enroll Now”, and our team will contact you with full details and batch timings.
