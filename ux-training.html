FAQs — Product Design (UX Design) Training by Trivikra Tech

Q1: Who is this course designed for?

This course is ideal for both beginners and experienced designers who want to master enterprise-level UX. It’s perfect for those aiming to work on complex products like CRM, POS, and ERP systems.

Q2: Can a complete fresher join this programme?

Yes, absolutely! The course starts with UX foundations and gradually builds up to advanced enterprise design concepts. No prior design experience is required.

Q3: What kind of products will I design during the course?

You’ll work on enterprise-grade applications such as CRM dashboards, POS systems, billing modules, analytics interfaces, and business management systems.

Q4: What makes this course different from a basic UI/UX course?

This programme focuses on enterprise UX — teaching you how to design large-scale, multi-role software systems used by companies, not just mobile apps or simple websites.

Q5: Who is the trainer for this programme?

The course is led by Ajinkya Kulthe, UX trainer and product designer at Trivikra Tech, with hands-on industry experience in enterprise product design.

Q6: How long is the course?

The programme runs for 8 weeks (2 months) with 60+ hours of live and guided sessions.

Q7: What is the mode of learning?

You can choose between Online or Hybrid modes depending on your schedule and location.

Q8: What will I learn each week?

You’ll start with system-level UX thinking and progress to Figma mastery, interaction design, usability testing, and a full capstone project with portfolio publishing.

Q9: Will I work on a real project?

Yes, each learner completes a Capstone Project — designing a complete CRM or enterprise application from wireframe to high-fidelity prototype.

Q10: How much time should I dedicate weekly?

On average, 6–8 hours per week (including live sessions, assignments, and project work) is recommended.

Q11: What tools will I learn in this course?

You’ll master Figma, FigJam, and Behance/Dribbble for creating, collaborating, and publishing enterprise design projects.

Q12: Do I need to know Figma before joining?

No prior Figma experience is needed. The programme includes a dedicated Figma Mastery Module covering Auto Layout, components, variants, and responsive systems.

Q13: Will I learn about design systems?

Yes, a full module is dedicated to Design Systems, where you’ll build scalable component libraries with tokens, documentation, and version control.

Q14: Does the course include data visualization and dashboard design?

Yes, you’ll design data-heavy interfaces like analytics dashboards, tables, filters, and graphs used in enterprise products.

Q15: Will I get a certificate after completing the course?

Yes, you’ll receive the Product Design Professional (Enterprise-Level) certification from Trivikra Tech.

Q16: Will I have a portfolio by the end of the programme?

Absolutely! You’ll graduate with professional case studies and interactive prototypes published on Behance and Dribbble.

Q17: Do you help with portfolio and LinkedIn optimization?

Yes, the final week includes portfolio review and LinkedIn optimization sessions to make you job-ready.

Q18: Is this course helpful for working professionals?

Definitely. It’s ideal for UI designers, graphic designers, or developers who want to transition into or advance within UX and product design roles.

Q19: What kind of career opportunities can I expect after completion?

You can apply for roles such as Product Designer, Enterprise UX Designer, Dashboard Designer, or Design System Specialist in tech and SaaS companies.

Q20: Does Trivikra Tech provide placement or career guidance?

Yes, Trivikra Tech offers career guidance, portfolio feedback, and industry mentorship to help you confidently enter product-based companies.
