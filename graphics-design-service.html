FAQs

Q1: What graphic design services do you offer?

We provide complete design solutions including logos, branding, social media graphics, brochures, and digital creatives for businesses of all sizes.

Q2: How does good graphic design help my business?

Professional design improves brand recognition, builds trust, and increases engagement across digital and print channels.

Q3: Do you offer full branding or just design?

Yes, we offer complete branding — from logo design to style guides — ensuring visual consistency across all brand touchpoints.

Q4: Can you create designs for both digital and print media?

Absolutely! We design for websites, social media, digital ads, as well as print materials like flyers, posters, and packaging.

Q5: What tools do you use for design projects?

Our team uses the latest tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, and Canva to deliver high-quality, scalable graphics.

Q6: How long does a typical design project take?

Timelines vary by project scope — simple logos take a few days, while full branding projects may take 1–3 weeks.

Q7: Will I receive the source files?

Yes, we provide editable source files (AI, PSD, or Figma) along with export formats like PNG, JPG, and PDF.

Q8: Do you include revisions in your design packages?

Yes, multiple revision rounds are included to ensure your design meets your brand vision perfectly.

Q9: Can you handle ongoing graphic design work?

Yes, we offer monthly or retainer-based design support for brands needing regular creative updates.

Q10: How can I start my graphic design project with Trivikra Tech?

Simply click "Explore Services" or reach out through our contact form — we’ll connect to discuss your brand goals and share a proposal.
