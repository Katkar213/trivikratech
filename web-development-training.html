FAQs – Front-End Development

Q1. I’m new to coding — can I join this program?

Yes! This bootcamp starts from basics and is perfect for complete beginners.

Q2. What projects will I build in this course?

You will build 5+ real projects including landing pages, React apps, and live deployed websites.

Q3. Are the classes live or recorded?

All classes are live and full recordings are provided for revision anytime.

Q4. Which tools and technologies will I learn?

HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript (ES6), React.js, GitHub, Git, and AI tools like ChatGPT & Copilot.

Q5. Will I learn how to deploy live websites?

Yes, you’ll deploy your projects on platforms like Netlify and Vercel.

Q6. Is job or freelancing support included?

Yes, we provide portfolio review, resume guidance, interview prep, and freelancing tips.

Q7. Can I join while studying or working?

Yes, flexible evening and weekend batches are available.

Q8. What are the system requirements?

A basic laptop with 8GB RAM and stable internet is enough to learn and code comfortably.

Q9. Will I get a certificate?

Yes, you’ll receive a Course Completion Certificate after completing the projects.

Q10. How do I enroll?

Click “Enroll Now” or “Get Free Counselling” and our team will guide you through the process.

Q11. What is the duration of this course?

The bootcamp runs for 2 months with live mentor-led classes and weekly projects.

Q12. Will I work on real client-style projects?

Yes, all assignments and projects are based on real-world scenarios.

Q13. What programming languages are included?

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and React.js for modern front-end development.

Q14. Will I learn how to build a portfolio?

Yes! You will create a complete developer portfolio showcasing your live projects.

Q15. How will doubts be solved?

Through live doubt sessions, 1:1 mentor help, and a private student support channel.

Q16. Can this course help me switch careers to IT?
