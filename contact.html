FAQs — Trivikra Tech Services & Professional Training

Q1: What services does Trivikra Tech provide?

We offer UI/UX design, website & app development, branding, digital marketing, usability audits, and professional UI/UX training programs.

Q2: I’m a startup — can you design my full product?

Yes, we handle complete product UX — user research, wireframes, UI design, design systems, prototype, and handover for development.

Q3: Do you provide redesign services?

Yes. We audit your current website/app, find usability problems, and redesign the entire product for better conversions and user experience.

Q4: How long does a UI/UX project take?

Simple projects take 3–7 days. Complete product design or multi-page websites take 2–4 weeks depending on complexity.

Q5: Do you also develop websites?

Yes, we develop responsive, fast, and SEO-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, JS, React, WordPress, or custom stacks based on your requirement.

Q6: Do you handle digital marketing?

Yes — we offer SEO, social media management, branding strategy, content writing, website optimization, and performance marketing.

Q7: What if I don’t know what design I want?

No problem. We guide you through brainstorming, competitor analysis, UI direction, and visual styles to find the perfect solution.

Q8: Will I get Figma files, assets, and handover?

Yes. You receive all source files including Figma, design systems, fonts, colors, icons, and developer-ready assets.

Q9: What if I need urgent delivery?

We provide express delivery options for urgent business requirements depending on project scope and availability.

Q10: Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer 30 days free support and optional monthly/annual maintenance plans for updates, fixes, and improvements.

Q11: Do you offer UI/UX training for beginners?

Yes — our training program is beginner-friendly and suitable for freshers, career switchers, and students with zero experience.

Q12: What tools are taught in the training?

Figma, FigJam, UX research tools, prototyping tools, usability testing platforms, and design system workflows.

Q13: Will I get a portfolio and case studies?

Yes. You will complete multiple case studies, app/web projects, and a professional Behance/portfolio website.

Q14: Do you provide placement support?

Yes — resume building, portfolio guidance, interview preparation, and recruiter connection assistance.

Q15: Are classes live or recorded?

Classes are live with recordings provided after every session for revision or missed classes.

Q16: What system do I need to join the training?

A laptop (8GB RAM recommended) with stable internet. Figma works even on basic systems.

Q17: Is this training suitable for freelancers?

Yes — we teach real client workflows, proposal writing, pricing, and how to close freelance projects confidently.

Q18: How long is the training duration?

The program lasts 3–4 months with complete step-by-step learning, assignments, and project building.

Q19: Do you give certificates after completion?

Yes — you receive a verified course completion certificate with project details and skill overview.

Q20: How do I enroll?

Click on “Enroll Now” or book a free counselling session. Our team will guide you with batch details and the course roadmap.
