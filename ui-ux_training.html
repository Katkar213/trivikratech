FAQs - Section 1

Q1. Do I need a design background to join?

No, this program starts from the basics and builds your skills step-by-step.

Q2. What projects will I complete in this course?

You will design 1 Mobile App, 1 Web UI Project, and 3 Complete Case Studies.

Q3. Are the classes live or recorded?

Classes are live and recordings are provided with lifetime access.

Q4. Which tools will I learn?

Figma, FigJam, Miro, Protopie, Maze, Zeplin, GitHub and Chrome DevTools.

Q5. Will I learn usability testing?

Yes, you will learn task-based testing and iteration improvements.

Q6. Is job or freelancing support included?

Yes, portfolio review, resume help, interview training and freelancing roadmap included.

Q7. Can I join while studying or working?

Yes, timings are flexible with evening and weekend batches.

Q8. What are the system requirements?

A laptop with stable internet connection is enough.

Q9. Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, you will get a course completion certificate after final project submission.

Q10. How do I enroll?

Click “Enroll Now” or “Get Free Counselling” and our team will contact you.

Q11. What is the duration of the course?

The complete course duration is 3–4 months depending on project pace.

Q12. Will I work on real client projects?

Yes, you will work with industry-style case studies and real scenarios.

Q13. What languages are used for UI development?

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap for responsive web UI development.

Q14. Will I learn how to create a portfolio?

Yes, you will create a Behance / personal portfolio website.

Q15. How are doubts handled?

Live doubt-solving, 1:1 mentor support and community chat support provided.

Q16. Can I shift to product design roles after this?

Yes, this program prepares you for UI/UX / Product Designer roles.

Q17. Will I learn Design Systems?

Yes, you will build components, tokens, variants, and scalable design systems.

Q18. Do I need drawing or sketching skills?

No, UI/UX is logic + user understanding based, not art based.

Q19. Will I learn real app workflows?

Yes, including dashboards, mobile flows, web app layouts, and micro-interactions.

Q20. Can I switch careers to UI/UX after this?

Yes, this course is beginner-friendly and structured for career transition.
